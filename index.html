Peachy

Peachy is a skateboard company who distributes skate products such as decks, merchandise, apparel, and accessories, with a based focus on the visual arts, as goods will reflect a variety of artworks from contemporary artists across different mediums.


Pushing in a Creative Direction.

With skateboarders supporting the artist community, we strive to build a stronger bond between these two social groups, and work together to visually create something new, for people of all ages, who can appreciate a range of art practices and the possible means of expression that can be taken away from them.
